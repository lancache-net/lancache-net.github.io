LAN Party game caching made easy

Make the most of your network. Get more play for your gamers. Download your games once and serve them out to many people at your LAN.

Get up and running in seconds.

Quick-Start Instructions

~ $ git clone https://github.com/lancachenet/docker-compose lancache

~ $ cd lancache

~/lancache $ nano .env

~/lancache $ docker-compose up -d

# => Configure your router to serve lancache-dns
